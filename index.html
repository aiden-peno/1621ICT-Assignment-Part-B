Welcome to Dolphin's Touch Football Club

About Us

Dolphin's Touch Football Club is a not for profit club run by a committee of volunteers for its members. The club has a proud history of running touch football along with an inclusive and family orientated club culture. It was founded in 1956 and has hosted a roster of both junior and senior touch football teams for the duration of its existence. The club currently fields six mixed junior teams and one senior mixed team, all of which hold a competitve position in their respective local leagues.

Our Mission

Dolphin's Touch Football Club will provide an inclusive, supportive family oriented environment for all of our members, players, and family. Our focus is on one Club for life and we will commit to the development of our players and the great game of Touch Football overall.

Our Vision

Our Club provides leadership, support, and direction to the development of our players and the game of Touch football in the South Brisbane region. We are backed by family values of the strongest foundations and we cultivate a nurturing culture that is supportive and inclusive for all Club players, members, and families. We believe that enjoyment of the game is as important as winning, and participation with one Club is a lifelong journey. We are commited to building up young people into better people.
